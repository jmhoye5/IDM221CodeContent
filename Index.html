is Xen

Xen is a Creative Developer, Photographer, Graphic Designer, Talent Manager, aspiring Creative Director, Producer, and student, based in Philadelphia / New York, working everywhere.

Jack Hoye, also known professionally as Xen, is an aspiring creative developer, director, and producer from the suburbs of New York City. From a young age, Xen dedicated much of his time to developing his style, objectives, and manifesto concerning media creation and the artistic process.

Moving to Philadelphia to pursue a Bachelor of Science at Drexel University proved to be invaluable as he was able to thrive as an artist amongst others for the first time. Ambitiously studying Entertainment & Arts Management and pursuing a concentration in Cinema & Television as well as an Interactive Digital Media minor Xen has experienced immense growth surrounding his creative sensibilities and artistic perception. Today, Xen practices photography, graphic design, and other visual art mediums, including film.

As a creative, Xen has been featured in numerous film festivals, including The 2017 All American High School Film Festival and Teen Indie Awards where his work was nominated for Best Edited Short. Xen is also published in numerous literary, art, and fashion magazines, including Soupstone Literary Magazine, Maya Literary Magazine, Paper Dragon Literary Journal, and Drexel University Design & Merchandising Magazine, showcasing acrylic paintings on canvas, photography, and poetry. Xen has recently contributed his graphic design skills, working alongside other artists on the digital fashion editorial, Droideka. And today, Xen talent manages the Philadelphia based progressive rock band, Glass Leaf.
